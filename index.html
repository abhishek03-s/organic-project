Organic fruits are becoming increasingly popular in India due to the growing awareness about the health and environmental benefits of organic farming practices. Many farmers in India are transitioning to organic farming methods to meet the growing demand for organic fruits and vegetables.

Organic fruits are often seen as a healthier alternative to conventionally grown fruits, as they contain higher levels of beneficial nutrients and lower levels of harmful chemicals. Organic farming practices also promote environmental sustainability by reducing pollution, conserving water, and protecting wildlife habitats.
