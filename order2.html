Orange

Oranges are a popular citrus fruit that are known for their sweet and tangy taste. They are also packed with nutrients and offer several health benefits, including:

  • High in Vitamin C: Oranges are an excellent source of Vitamin C, which is important for a healthy immune system and can also help protect against chronic disease.
  • Good for Heart Health: Oranges contain flavonoids and potassium, which can help lower blood pressure, reduce inflammation, and improve overall heart health.
  • Digestive Health: Oranges are high in fiber, which can help promote regularity and prevent constipation.
  • Anti-inflammatory: Oranges contain antioxidants and anti-inflammatory compounds, which can help protect against chronic inflammation and reduce the risk of chronic disease..
